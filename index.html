About Me

I'm a 19-year-old Moroccan studying Data Engineering at École Supérieure de Technologie à Al Aasraghna. My passion for technology ignited when I was 14 years old, and I've been on an incredible journey of learning and creating ever since.

My interests span across algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and web development. I'm currently focused on mastering data structures, algorithms, machine learning, and large language models to build innovative solutions that make a real impact.

Recently, I had the honor of winning HACKATHON'25 (OpenData), where my team and I developed a cutting-edge solution that showcased our technical skills and problem-solving abilities. This achievement reinforced my belief that technology can solve complex real-world problems.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring the latest AI trends, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring fellow students in programming fundamentals.
